
Knowledge Check


What are two benefits of having well-written commit messages and a good commit history?

Gives a concise and complete history of an project. Well explained commit messages provide context and history to all of the commits that are made for the project.

Enhances readability when the projects commits are under review. 

How many characters should the subject line of your commit message be?

At max, the subject line should be no longer than 72 characters, but 50 is a really good guideline.
